The project will continue abroad in the prestigious exhibition venues of the Italian Cultural Institutes.

The exhibitions present works of art executed with different artistic techniques including: painting, sculpture, modern and cosmatesque mosaic, glass work, ceramics, plastic, fashion, design and digital art. All the works are created using the compositional materials
Egyptian blue.
The exhibitions are coordinated and curated by BLUENET and the scientific committee.
